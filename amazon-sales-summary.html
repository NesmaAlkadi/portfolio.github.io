Amazon Sales Project

Data Cleaning, Modeling, and Visualization using Power BI.

This project involved analyzing Amazon sales data in India to uncover insights into sales trends, product performance, and customer feedback. The entire workflow, from data cleaning to visualization, was conducted in Power BI.

Steps Taken:

Data Cleaning:

  • Removed unnecessary rows and columns, reducing the dataset from 1,466 rows to 1,186 rows.
  • Standardized price columns by removing currency symbols and converting them to decimal format.
  • Split detailed columns into meaningful categories:
    • Product Name split into Brand and Product Name.
    • Category split into Main Category and subcategories.
  • Simplified review data by extracting key parts of reviews (titles and content).
  • Removed irrelevant data like image and product links.

Data Modeling:

  • Created DAX Measures to calculate:
    • Total Brands
    • Total Products
    • Total Revenue
    • Total Reviews
    • Average Discount Percentage
    • Average Rating
  • Added custom columns for deeper insights:
    • Rating Label (e.g., "Bad", "Average", "Good", "Excellent").
    • Rating Bin for grouping ratings (e.g., 0–1, 1–2, etc.).
    • Discount Range for categorizing discount percentages.

Dashboard Design:

  • Total Products:

    1,186

  • Total Brands:

    382

  • Average Rating:

    4

  • Categories slicer:

    Displays the main category, subcategory 1, subcategory 2, and subcategory 3

  • Top 5 Brands by Average Rating:

    Highlights the highest-rated brands based on customer reviews

  • Sum of Discounted Prices by Discount Range:

    Displays the distribution of products based on discount percentages

  • Total Revenue by Main Category:

    Electronics and Home & Kitchen generate the most revenue

  • Rating Distribution:

    Displays the number of products in different rating ranges (e.g., 0–1, 1–2) across categories

  • Detailed Products:

    Highlights specific products along with their brands, descriptions, and other details

Key Insights:

  • Identified top-performing brands and products.
  • Explored the impact of discounts on revenue.
  • Analyzed customer satisfaction through ratings and reviews.
